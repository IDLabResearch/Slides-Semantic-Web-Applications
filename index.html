Smart queries work with Linked Data

  • All data items consist of 3 parts

    • subject
    • predicate (verb)
    • object

  • Every part has a URI

    • http://rdf.freebase.com/ns/en.orlando_bloom

  • Every URI can have links

    • they are typed links: http://rdf.freebase.com/ns/film.film.starring

Search engines launched schema.org

  • Who?

    A joint effort by Google, Bing (Microsoft), and Yahoo!

  • What?

    A schema that describes what webpages are about

  • Why?

    Allows you to express the meaning of your content

  • How?

    Using HTML microdata.
